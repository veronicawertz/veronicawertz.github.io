Leadership Experience

Cornell University | Pole Posse

Pole Posse is a dance club at Cornell. Shortly after joining the club, I handled the finances and scheduling as Treasurer from January 2019 until January 2020. As Treasurer, I also helped organize a charity performance to raise over $1,500, through ticket sales, for Planned Parenthood. I started co-teaching dance workshops 2-3 times a week for current members and Cornell students in the Spring of 2019 as well. Later in the fall of 2019, I worked together with the rest of the club's leadership to obtain a sponsorship from X-Pole, one of the leading aerial arts equipment companies. That same fall, I organized an additional showcase to raise money for new club equipment. For this show, I booked rooms, organized practices, and managed ticket sales. As of January 2020, I manage all communications between the club and the Cornell community.

Cornell University | Pre-freshman Summer Program Teaching Assistant

As a teaching assistant for the Pre-freshman Summer Program, I provided math instruction for a Chemistry course for incoming freshmen. I assisted a group of approximately 40 students in understanding the mathematical concepts needed to be successful in chemistry. Additionally, I helped write questions for in-class quizzes and graded weekly quizzes, entrance exams, and final exams. I improved my communication skills by learning how to effectively explain concepts to students. I belive taking on this role allowed me to further develop my leadership skills by practicing working with others and communicating my ideas.
